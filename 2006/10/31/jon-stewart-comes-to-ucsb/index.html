Jon Stewart Comes to UCSB

Date Tue 31 October 2006

Wednesday morning’s Daily Nexus filled my body with excitement as the title of the article reads, “Comedy Central Host Jon Stewart To Perform Live.” That’s correct Jon Stewart is coming to UCSB and guess who got a ticket? Scott and I!

After the second night of partying for Halloween I managed to wake at 7:00AM and rally the troops so that we could get tickets for this glorious event. The tickets on sale Saturday morning were discounted and only offered to UCSB students. Some may consider our seats to be of inferior quality, but when it comes to Jon Stewart there are no bad seats, and especially considering for non UCSB students tickets range from $70-500; I’d say we got a sweet deal.
